413 Payload Too Large

The request entity is larger than limits defined by the server.

Class: 4xx Client Error Cacheable: no Safe to retry: no
Advertisement

When you see HTTP 413

Uploading a file or body bigger than the server/proxy allows.

How to fix 413 Payload Too Large

Increase client_max_body_size (nginx) or the relevant body limit, or compress/chunk the upload.
